A Basic Understanding of Calculus

Welcome to Intro to Calculus the purpose of this app is to help those who are struggling with the basics of calculus, and to introduce those who are interested in calculus to gain a basic understanding of what Calculus is.

In this app, we will be focusing on:

  • Algebra
  • Functions
  • Limits
  • Derivatives
  • Antiderivatives and Integrals
